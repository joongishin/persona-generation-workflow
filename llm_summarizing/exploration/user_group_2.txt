This user group is composed of single, educated individuals in their 30s and 50s who value creativity, personal growth, and learning. They engage in activities such as attending cultural events, practicing yoga, and reading books to expand their knowledge and maintain overall wellness. Most of the members of this group are currently unsatisfied with their lifestyle and express the desire to have children in the future for a deeper sense of fulfillment and purpose. They appreciate and expect civic services to prioritize accessible, affordable, and high-quality childcare, education, and cultural enrichment programs. As a whole, this user group seeks a well-rounded, enriching lifestyle and support in achieving meaningful personal growth and family life.
