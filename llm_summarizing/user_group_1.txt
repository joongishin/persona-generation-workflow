In this user group, we have a diverse set of individuals mostly in their late 20s and early 30s who prioritize personal growth and career advancement, while also cherishing relationships and their own health and wellbeing. They enjoy various activities such as attending events related to their careers, participating in sports, practicing mindfulness, and exploring new experiences. Most of the group members express the desire to have children someday, but not at the current time, as they are focused on their current lifestyles and careers. When considering raising children, they value access to affordable childcare, quality education, healthcare services, and recreational programs or spaces. Overall, this user group is defined by their dedication to personal improvement, lasting relationships, and plans for future families.
